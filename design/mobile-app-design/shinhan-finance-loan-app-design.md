## **Client Requirements**

* Clean, modern interface
* Must stand out from competitors
* Close collaboration with the Customer Experience Manager

## **Work Process**

* Remote job with **twice-weekly office visits**
* Meetings to define features and specifications
* Initial sketches on paper to refine layout and understanding

## **Wireframe and Layout Development**

* **Provided Materials**
  * Wireframe with flow and layout for the mobile version
  * Requirement to follow mobile wireframe while designing desktop version
*   **Challenges**

    1. **Understanding Wireframe & Flow** – Detailed discussions needed to ensure clarity
    2. **Simultaneous Mobile & Desktop Design** – No dedicated desktop wireframe, requiring close adaptation from mobile
