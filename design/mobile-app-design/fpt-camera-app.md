# FPT Camera App

## **Project Scope**

Designed key functionalities for the **FPT Camera App** and **FPT Camera Playbox**.

## **Responsibilities**

* Collaborated with the product team to design new features for the applications.
* Created functional flows ensuring usability and development feasibility.
* Participated in meetings with the product and development teams to implement designs effectively.

## **Challenges**

* The initial application had various issues, including bugs and inefficiencies.
* Overlapping workflows caused delays and made feature implementation complex and time-consuming.

## **Solutions**

* Focused on delivering well-designed, functional features.
* Continuously optimized user flows and worked closely with stakeholders to streamline implementation.
