# 1NK: Manga NFT Platform

## **Overview**

Designed a platform for digitizing manga into NFT art.

## **Responsibilities**

* Designed based on platform functionalities.
* Created UI/UX following wireframes.
* Collaborated with PMs, developers, mangaka, and founders to transform the product.

## **Challenges**

* Adapting quickly to evolving requirements.
* Maintaining consistency with the existing website while introducing unique elements.

## **Solutions**

* Structured and optimized platform functionalities.
* Researched and analyzed similar platforms to integrate the best features.
* Maintained close collaboration with stakeholders.
* Developed multiple design solutions to explore different possibilities.

## **Takeaways**

* Gained experience in designing for NFT and blockchain-related platforms.
* Improved adaptability to fast-changing project requirements.
* Strengthened collaboration skills with cross-functional teams.
* Enhanced ability to balance innovation with existing design constraints.
