# How I Maintain and Improve My Work

## Tools

I rely on various design tools to improve my design process and streamline workflows. To keep track of these, I bookmark tools based on their purpose—whether for creativity, productivity, or automation. This makes it easy to revisit and implement them when needed.

## **Folder Structure**

Maintaining a well-organized folder system is crucial for quick access to files, both for myself and my team. I use clear and logical naming conventions to ensure we can locate previous work or resources without hassle.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screen Shot 2024-12-13 at 21.24.03.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## **Figma Files**

I always organize styles, components, and guidelines in Figma to make them easy to find and understand for anyone working with the file. This ensures consistency and simplifies collaboration across the team.
