# AI-Enhanced Design Workflow

## Context & Knowledge Building

I use NotebookLM, Gemini, Claude, and ChatGPT to organize all project information. By creating a custom dataset, I ensure the AI understands the project deeply and can provide accurate answers and insights.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (11).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Research & Inspiration

I explore AI tools and design websites to find creative ideas and study competitors. I pick out the best and most unique concepts to use as a foundation for my work.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-04-05 at 13.53.41.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Concept Design

I turn those ideas into multiple design versions. By mixing and matching different elements, I refine them until I reach the most effective and high-quality result.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-04-05 at 13.57.50.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Testing & Refinement

I use AI tools and gather feedback from real users, teammates, and clients. I continue to test and improve the design until it is polished and meets all goals.
