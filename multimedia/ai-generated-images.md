# AI Generated Images

## **Receive the Request**

* Receive the client or team request or define the image needs.
* Analyze the requirements: style, theme, colors, and intended use.

<details>

<summary>Sample request</summary>

The TonGYM Community is Open!

**Theme**: Exciting, community-driven, and celebratory, with a focus on TonGYM’s exclusive weekly giveaways.

**Elements to Include**:

1. **Title**: Weekly Giveaways
2. **Core Details**:

* **Launch Date**: "Starting Friday, Nov 15th, 2024"
* **Prize**: "$100 Prize Pool Weekly"
* **Winners**: "10 Random Winners from the Top 100"

**Call to Action**: "Join the Community!" with link [https://t.me/tonhealthPortal](https://t.me/tonhealthPortal) or QR code

**Visual Style**:

* TonGYM brand colors.
* Incorporate visuals related to fitness, competition, or rewards.

Howie Nguyen (Guest) DDL: Nov 13th

&#x20;

</details>

## **Research Real Images**

* Find reference images related to the request.
* Gather relevant elements such as composition, lighting, colors, and details.
* Identify the desired artistic style (realistic, anime, digital painting, etc.).

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/future-artificial-intelligence-robot-adult-technology-futuristic.png" alt="" width="188"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

## **Use AI Tools to Generate the Image**

* Input the prompt into the AI tool to generate an image.
* If the output is not satisfactory, adjust the prompt and regenerate.
* Utilize advanced features (custom styles, inpainting, upscaling) to enhance quality.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screen Shot 2025-02-28 at 22.29.42.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## **Edit and Finalize**

* Review the image details and ensure there are no errors or inconsistencies.
* If needed, refine the image using Photoshop, Illustrator, or other editing tools.
* Export the file in the required format and resolution.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/The TonGYM Community is Open!.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## **Delivery and Feedback**

* Send the final image to the client or integrate it into the project.
* Collect feedback and make adjustments if necessary.
