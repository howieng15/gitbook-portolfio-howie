# Banner

## Swan Chain

Swan Chain is a key client of Helix Mesh, and I served as the lead designer for all their social media content, including banners, [videos](video-and-animation.md), and GIFs.

Their X page: [https://x.com/swan\_chain](https://x.com/swan_chain)

### **Receive the Brief**

The team provides the brief, content, and deadline, I start working on it and always deliver within a day.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screen Shot 2025-03-09 at 10.29.42.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### **Research Concepts**

I explore social media platforms for inspiration, looking at trending designs or ideas that fit the brief.

&#x20;![](<../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-12-05 at 17.28.45 (2).png>)

### **Gather Resources**

I search for high-quality design resources, such as icons, illustrations, or stock images, on platforms like Freepik.&#x20;

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-12-05 at 17.29.29.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

### **Design in Figma**

Using Figma, I create the banner. I work on the layout, typography, and other visual elements, ensuring everything aligns with the brief and delivers an engaging design.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Group 403126.jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### **Follow Brand Guidelines**

Ensure the banner adheres to the brand’s guidelines. This includes using the correct fonts, colors, and imagery to maintain brand consistency and professionalism.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-12-03 at 10.21.5621.jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## TonGym

TonGym is a client of Helix Mesh, and I supported them in creating social media posts for X and Telegram. I primarily used AI-generated 3D-style art to align with their brand aesthetic.

Their X page: [https://x.com/TonGYMbot](https://x.com/TonGYMbot)

Their website: [https://tongym.io](https://tongym.io/)

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screen Shot 2025-03-09 at 09.55.11 (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Receive Brief

The team provides the brief, content, and deadline, I start working on it and always deliver within a day.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screen Shot 2025-03-09 at 10.38.42.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
