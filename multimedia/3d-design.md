# 3D Design

## C**oncept**

I start by getting a concept - either from a client, a project brief, or through my own research. The concept needs to be interesting, visually appealing, and something that pushes my skills forward.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/3d1.jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## **Model in Maya**

Once I have the concept, I dive into Maya to create the 3D model. I focus on the structure, keeping the geometry clean and the shapes accurate to bring the idea to life.

<div><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/3d2.jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/3d3.jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/3d4.jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

## **Check UV**

Use UV texture to check pixel perfect.

<div><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/3d5.jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/3d6.jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

## **Texturing**

After modeling, I move on to applying textures. I make sure the materials and colors fit the concept and enhance the overall aesthetic of the model.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/3d9.jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## **Render** occlusion

Use occlusion to render shadow for texture.
